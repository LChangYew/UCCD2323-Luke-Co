Frequently Asked Questions ?

How do I track my order? +
Once your order has been packed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our Luke&Co-shipping info website or the courier's website.
What is your company return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items with having original tags attached.
How can I change or cancel my order? +
You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After that time, your order will be processed for shipping.
Do your companny ship internationally? +
Yes. Our company provides worldwide delivery services, and customers outside of our home country can place orders with ease. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on the destination, but we ensure that your package is handled securely and arrives in a time.
